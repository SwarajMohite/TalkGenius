## 🧠 About TalkGenius  

TalkGenius is a **unified AI-powered communication platform** designed to help people of all age groups improve their **confidence, clarity, and consistency**.

From interview preparation to debate practice and real-time feedback, it creates a **daily practice ecosystem** where growth becomes measurable and engaging.


---

## Why TalkGenius

- **Confidence-first learning:** Move from hesitation to clear, confident speaking.
- **Practice + feedback loop:** Learn by doing, then improve with actionable insights.
- **Consistency-driven design:** Daily tracking and progress systems encourage regular practice.
- **Unified platform:** Multiple communication growth modules in one place.

---

## Core Product Experience

- **Practice Mirror** for guided speaking sessions.
- **AI Roleplay** for realistic conversation simulations.
- **AI Debate** for argument-building and critical thinking.
- **Inspiration Analyzer** to learn from high-impact speakers.
- **Lab Simulation** for scenario-based communication training.
- **Dashboard & Progress Tracking** with streaks, time analytics, and leaderboard.

---

## Product Capabilities

- **Authentication:** Email/password + Google sign-in.
- **Personalized dashboard:** Module access, live timer, and progress overview.
- **Progress intelligence:** Local + cloud-synced tracking for learning continuity.
- **Leaderboard system:** Community motivation through ranking and activity.
- **Modern UX:** Responsive, theme-aware interface built for focus.

---

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** Next.js, React, TypeScript
- **UI:** Tailwind CSS, Radix UI, Lucide Icons, Framer Motion
- **Auth:** Firebase Authentication
- **Data:** Supabase (progress, users, leaderboard)
- **Charts/Insights:** Recharts
